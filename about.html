About St. Mary's

Nurturing Excellence

Our Vision

Everyone in our school community deserves to be cared for unconditionally and valued equally as a unique creation, made in the image of God.

In our learning, in our work, in our relationships and in the ways in which we organise our community we are committed to:

  1. A set of spiritual and moral values – honesty, integrity and good judgement.
  2. A complement of basic skills – linguistic, mathematical, scientific, artistic, physical and social.
  3. An enquiring and discriminating mind and a desire for knowledge.
  4. Strong self-esteem and high personal expectation.
  5. Tolerance and respect for others.

We value the partnership which exists between school, parents and the community for the part it plays in realising this vision.

Our Mission

The primary objective of the Institution is to bring about the harmonious blending between traditional and progressive methods of education. Our goal is to instil into these young minds the moral and ethical values, good character, discipline, dedication, national outlook, firm faith in all religions and an abiding spirit of service and sacrifice.

Through this, we hope to encourage them to develop a broader and healthier outlook in life as well as to build a strong sovereign, socialist and secular democracy. In short, our aim is to impart the best education with utmost care, love and understanding.
